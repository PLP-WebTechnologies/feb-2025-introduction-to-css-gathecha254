Whispers of the Infinite

Jake had always been fascinated by the stars. As a child, he would sneak onto the rooftop, staring up at the endless sky, dreaming of exploring the universe. One evening, as he gazed at the constellations, a bright streak of light shot across the sky. It wasn’t just a meteor—it was something different. His heart pounded as a soft hum filled the air, and a mysterious glow appeared behind the trees. Curiosity took over, and without a second thought, he ran toward the light, unaware that his life was about to change forever.
